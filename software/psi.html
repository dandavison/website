Population structure inference

psi: parallel structure inference [contact me if you want to use this]

This software fits the 'admixture model' described by Pritchard et al. (2000) Genetics (2):945-959, to SNP genotype data. The model may be fitted using either MCMC (as described by the original authors), or the EM algorithm. The EM fit may be carried out in parallel on a compute cluster with some version of MPI installed. In that case the speed-up is roughly linear in the number of processors allocated to the task.
